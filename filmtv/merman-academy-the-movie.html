Merman Academy: The Movie (2020 film)

Merman Academy: The Movie is a 2020 American comedy fantasy family film written, directed, produced, and edited by Zeke Taylor and Jabari Leggette. It stars Zeke Taylor, Immoney Cooper, Jabari Leggette, Earnest Harper, and Malachi Taylor, and is narrated by Zeke Taylor. Merman Academy: The Movie was globally released on June 24th, 2020 by Balckflix Productions.
