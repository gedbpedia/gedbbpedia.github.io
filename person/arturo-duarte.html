Arturo Duarte

Arturo Duarte (born July 1, 2004) is a Portuguese social entrepreneur, social media marketer and social media manager. He is best known for his work of social entrepreneurship and online work of social media marketing and managing.

Early life

Arturo Duarte was born on July 1, 2004. He grew up in Spain and moved to Portugal around 7 years ago at age nine. Growing up, Duarte had always been moving around the world and then he finally settled for Portugal. Around five years ago, when he was about 11, Arturo's parents divorced.

Career

After settling in Portugal, Duarte discovered how the internet could change how industries work and can be used to bring more peace and positivity in the world and that's what he decided to do. In 2018, he started studying management as a way to began and he currently is still. Soon he started managing several amounts of social networks, but then Duarte decided to create his own network, shop, and organization on the 7th of August in 2019. Arturo Duarte is currently the CEO of the esports organization Team9K and is he also happens to be the youngest entrepreneur as of right now to become chief executive officer.

Personal life

In his spare time, Arturo Duarte likes to do his favorite hobbies and activities. In 2016, Duarte started skateboarding as an activity and he currently still enjoys doing it. In 2017, he started surfing as another and along with skateboarding does it frequently as well still. Other times, Duarte said he likes to hang out with his friends and go out.
