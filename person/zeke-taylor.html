Zeke Taylor

Zeke Taylor (born December 29, 2005) is an American Actor and Internet Personality born in Little Rock, Arkansas. He became a notable personality after starring in various big-hit projects.

Cause of Fame

As the actor said himself, he first garnered fame and success after starring in the hit web-television series “Merman Academy”.
